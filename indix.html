FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does international shipping take?

Shipping times vary by service: Sea Freight takes 20-45 days, Air Freight 3-7 days, Land Freight 5-15 days, and Express 1-3 days. Exact times depend on origin, destination, and customs processing.

What items can I ship internationally?

We can ship most commercial goods including electronics, textiles, machinery, food products, and more. We also handle hazardous materials (DGR) with proper certification. Some items may be restricted based on destination country regulations.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes! We provide complete customs clearance services at all major ports including Lattakia, Tartous, Beirut, and Aqaba. Our experienced team handles all documentation, duty calculations, and regulatory compliance to ensure smooth clearance.

Is my cargo insured?

We offer comprehensive cargo insurance options for all shipments. Basic coverage is included, and you can opt for full-value insurance for complete protection. Our insurance partners cover loss, damage, and delays.

How do I get a shipping quote?

Getting a quote is easy! Fill out our online request form, call us at 011-2158820, or send an email. We need your cargo details (weight, dimensions, type), origin, destination, and preferred shipping method to provide an accurate quote within 24 hours.

Can I track my shipment?

Absolutely! Every shipment gets a unique tracking number. Use our online tracking system or mobile app to monitor your cargo in real-time. You'll receive automatic notifications at key milestones including pickup, departure, arrival, and delivery.
